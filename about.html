About Us

The PWD ID Registration System of Cagayan de Oro is designed to provide a more accessible, efficient, and user-friendly way for Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) to register and apply for their PWD Identification Card online. The website aims to simplify the registration process by allowing users to submit requirements, access important information, and stay updated on their application status in one convenient platform.

Through digital accessibility and responsive public service, the system supports the city's commitment to inclusion, equal opportunity, and better service for the PWD community.

Vision & Mission

Vision

An inclusive, universally accessible, and digitally empowered Cagayan de Oro City where every Person with Disability (PWD) seamlessly enjoys their rights, benefits, and welfare.

Mission

To provide an efficient, barrier-free online registration system that simplifies public service delivery, ensures data accuracy, and actively promotes the digital inclusion of the CDO PWD community.

Powered by CMISID — ICT solutions for inclusive public service

The RISE Framework Integration

This portal is a flagship digital component of Cagayan de Oro's RISE Framework (Regional Infrastructure and Sustainable Ecosystem). By moving the PWD ID application process online, the city aims to eliminate physical, geographical, and bureaucratic barriers for our constituents.

Agencies

Persons with Disability Affairs Office (PDAO) — CDO

The primary office responsible for evaluating submitted documents, managing the local PWD registry, and advocating for local welfare programs.

City Social Welfare and Development Department (CSWDD)

The localized department handles the production, verification, and distribution of the physical PWD ID cards.

City Management Information Systems and Innovation Department (CMISID)

The technical unit responsible for hosting the server infrastructure, deploying portal updates, and maintaining system security.

Legal & Privacy

Legal Mandates

Republic Act No. 7277 (Magna Carta for Disabled Persons): Grants the fundamental rights, privileges, and 20% discounts for verified PWDs.

Republic Act No. 10754: Expands PWD benefits to include Value Added Tax (VAT) exemptions on critical goods and services.

Data Privacy Statements

In accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), the City Government of Cagayan de Oro guarantees that all sensitive personal information, medical files, and identification documents uploaded to this portal are strictly encrypted. Your data is accessible only to authorized social workers and evaluation officers during verification.
